Introduction

This is my final project for COMP2019 (Virtual Communications). The purpose of this website is to communicate the history of women's hockey in a web-friendly manner using topics we have learned about in class.

Pages and what they cover

Early History

This page covers early women's hockey history from the late nineteenth century to the 1960s when women's hockey started taking off.

International Growth

This page covers international championships in women's hockey and gives a brief overview of historical statistics.

Professional Leagues

This page covers the history of professional and semi-professional women's hockey and the challenges that have come up in terms of building a lasting league.

What this website covers

This website covers the history and development of women's hockey, spanning from the early beginnings in the late nineteenth century to its growth today as an international sport. It highlights both successes and challenges during the growth of the game, as well as highlighting key events, players, and teams. Overall, it aims to provide an informative overview on the evolution of the sport.

Target Audience

The main target audience for this page is students and new hockey fans looking for a broad overview of the history of women's hockey.

Example Persona

  • Age: 18-23
  • Education: college student
  • Program: Sports Management
  • Background: A college student who is a fan of hockey in general but is less familiar with women's hockey. They are looking for a starting point for an assignment they have on the history of the sport and the challenges it has faced during its growth.
  • Motivation for visiting the site: They have found that most information is scattered and that it is difficult to find a clear overview of information that links to further resources when required.
